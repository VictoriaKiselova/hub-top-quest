How to Play

  • Swipe to match three or more items of the same color to clear them from the board

  • Complete specific objectives, such as collecting items or reaching target scores, to finish each level

  • Use power-ups and special tools strategically to overcome difficult challenges and maximize your score

  • Plan your moves carefully, as some levels may have limited moves or time constraints

  • Advance through increasingly challenging levels, each with unique objectives and obstacles

  • Join time-limited events for additional rewards and exclusive gameplay opportunities
